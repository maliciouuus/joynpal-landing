# JoynPal — Brand Guide

## Company
**Next Level International Limited** (Hong Kong)

## App Name
**JoynPal** — Joy + Join + Pal

## Tagline
"Rent a Friend. Anywhere. Anytime."

## Logo
- `logo.svg` — Full wordmark (Joyn in indigo, Pal in amber)
- `logo-icon.svg` — App icon (two connected figures on indigo background)

## Colors

### Primary
- **Indigo** — #4F46E5 (trust, tech, premium)
- **Indigo Light** — #6366F1
- **Indigo Dark** — #3730A3

### Secondary
- **Amber** — #F59E0B (warmth, friendship, energy)
- **Amber Light** — #FBBF24

### Neutral
- **Dark** — #0F172A
- **Gray 900** — #1E293B
- **Gray 500** — #64748B
- **Gray 100** — #F1F5F9
- **White** — #FFFFFF

### Semantic
- **Green** — #10B981 (success, verified)
- **Red** — #EF4444 (error, danger)

## Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** 800-900 weight, tight letter-spacing
- **Body:** 400-500 weight

## Voice & Tone
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Warm** but professional
- **Direct** — no fluff
- **Inclusive** — everyone deserves connection

## Usage Rules
- "Joyn" is always indigo, "Pal" is always amber
- Minimum logo size: 24px height
- Always maintain clear space around logo
- Dark backgrounds: use white wordmark with colored icon
